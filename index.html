HAGIN BOOKS I HAVE READ

HOW YOU CAN BE LED BY THE SPIRIT OF GOD.

How You Can Be Led by The Spirit Of God by Kenneth E. Hagin is a Step by step guidance through the Scriptures to steer Christians away from spiritual pitfalls and help them to follow the Spirit of God in every area of life. The conscience, the inward witness, prophecy, visions, and training the human spirit.

GROWING UP SPIRITUALLY

As believers, we all begin as spiritual babes in Christ. But just as we grow in the natural, we are also to grow up spiritually. Kenneth E. Hagin lays out the character traits of each stage of spiritual growth and shares how we can locate ourselves. He explains that when we feed on the right things, we will advance to the next level of development. Learn what it takes to grow up in God, to grow up spiritually!

LOVE THE WAY TO VICTORY

In Love: The Way to Victory, Rev. Hagin shows why God's love is so vital to our lives. He teaches how to let the love of God dominate our lives rather than to allow our flesh or our unredeemed thinking to rule us. So embark on God's great quest for your life and learn how you can turn around even what seem to be impossible situations in your life---just by walking in the God-kind of love.

THE ART OF PRAYER

God is calling for those who will give themselves to prayer! Containing teachings on many important facets of prayer, The Art of Prayer will serve as an invaluable handbook on prayer not only for individual believers, but also for prayer groups and churches!

FOLLOWING GOD'S PLAN FOR YOUR LIFE

In Following God's Plan for Your Life, Rev. Kenneth E. Hagin presents profound teaching on how to discover and fulfill the divine destiny God has ordained for your life. Learn how you can follow God's plan for your life and stay on the course He has set before you!

PLANS, PURPOSES AND PURSUITS

Learn how to lay aside fleshly plans and pursuits to experience the true worship of God!
